Recipe Name: Chocolate Chip Cookies
Description: Delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies.
Ingredients:
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs
- 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a small bowl, mix the flour and baking soda; set aside.
3. In a large bowl, cream the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and salt together until smooth.
4. Beat in the vanilla extract and eggs until the mixture is creamy.
5. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients.
6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
7. Drop rounded tablespoons of the dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
8. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until the edges are golden brown.
9. Cool on wire racks.
